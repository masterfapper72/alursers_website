FAQ

WHAT IS AN NFT?

NFTs ("Non-Fungible Tokens") are one-of-a-kind tokens that represent a unique good or asset, like digital art. They can be sold to collectors on various platforms, as well as they can be bought.



HOW TO BUY ALURSERS NFTs?

You can buy our NFTs in 3 different ways: the first one is to buy them through the private sale that we’ll be doing on Solanium. Then you can buy them through one of the private sales that we’ll be hosting on our website. At last, you can trade NFTs on marketplaces such as Solsea.



WHERE DO I FIND MY NFT?

Take the phantom wallet as an example, you can find your NFT in the assets section.



WHAT IS A BLOOB AND WHAT CAN I DO WITH IT?

You can look up all the infos you need by looking at our litepaper! You can find it in the “Roadmap” section of our webiste.



WHERE CAN I SEE PROJECT DEVELOPEMENTS?

We will be sharing our project’s developements mostly on Twitter and our Telegram channel. You can find the links directly in the main page of the site, by clicking on the Twitter and Telegram icons.



HOW CAN I CONTRIBUTE TO THE PROJECT?

You can contribute to this and future projects by telling us your own ideas on the dedicated Telegram account and through the voting system (you can find more infos in the Litepaper). We will then choose the best ideas to be part of the Alursers future!



WHAT IF AN ADMIN CONTACTS ME?

Scam. And we couldn’t be more clear. We will never contact you first. If you are in doubt, contact us first so we’ll try to help you out.



YOU DIDN’T FIND YOUR ANSWER?

If you didn’t find the answer to your question, write an email to info@alursers.tech and we’ll answer as soon as possible!
